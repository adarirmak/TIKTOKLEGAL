Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tiktok Bot handles information when using the TikTok Content Posting API.

Information We Collect

We only access the TikTok OAuth access token granted by the account owner. No personal data from other TikTok users is collected or stored.

How We Use Information

The access token is used solely to upload pre-created video content to the authorized TikTok account. Tokens are stored locally on the operator's device only.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

This application interacts with the TikTok API. TikTok's own privacy policy applies to all data processed by TikTok.
